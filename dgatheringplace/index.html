Kunu Drink

Ingredients

  • 1.2g of Millet or guniea Corn
  • Tigernut (For Milky flavour)
  • 1 cup of Dates(Natural Sweetener)
  • Sweet Potatoes: Either Dried/ fresh (Reduces the thickness)
  • Ginger
  • Garlic Cloves/ Cinnamon

Preparation

  • Measure 10 (250g cups) of millet into a pot, with 1 cup of dates (Remove the seeds), and 1 cup of tigernut. Leave it to soak for about 8 Hours
  • Wrinse your millets, dates, and tigernut well, then pour them gradually into your blender. Add a bit of water to your blender to ease the blending process.
  • After blending, get a large pot and pour your millet mix to the pot.
  • Put your gas on medium heat and stirr to prevent lumps. NB: It takes time to thicken, just keep stirring.
  • After it has thickened to a desired consostency, turn of the heat and leave to coli.
  • Add your sugar, or honey if prefferd
  • Add iced block pellets to the kunu, or leave in the refrigerator
  • Enjoy your fresh kunu!!!

Chapman

Ingredients

  • Orange Juice of any kind (fanta, mirinda, orange wine, e.t.c.)
  • Clia Drink
  • Apple Drink if available
  • Grinadine (For cliouring)
  • Lime (Adds a subtle taste to the mixture)
  • Lemon
  • Fruits for garnishing (Cucumber, pineapples, strawberry, lemon, orange, e.t.c.)

Preparation

  • Measure 4 - 6 bottles of orange and clia juice into a bowl
  • Slice your lemons and squeeze into a bowl
  • Slice some lemons to thin strips and leave in the bowl. It would be used for garnishing later
  • Dice your pineapples for taste
  • Blend your pineapples, then sieve the juice witha cheese cloth.
  • Add the pineapple juice to the bowl
  • Refrigerate and enjoy!!!

Ginger & Pineapple Juice

Ingredients

  • Ginger
  • Pineapple
  • Lemon

Preparation

  • Wash your ginger very well, no need of peeling it.
  • You can either wash with salt or sponge
  • Peel your pineapple and chop it into chunks to ease blending
  • Chop your unpeeled ginger too, and blend your ginger first
  • The add your pineapples after blending your ginger
  • Pour the juice into a bowl and sieve with a cheese cloth.
  • Add powdered pineapple flavour
  • Add lemons and remove the seeds.
  • Enjoy!!!

Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • Flour 500g (4 cups)
  • Sugar 1 cup
  • Buter 50g
  • Yeast 1tbsp
  • Eggs 1 (No matter the amount or quantity of flour, your eggs should be 1.)
  • Milk 50g

    Ingredients for pizza toppings

  • Hot dogs
  • Bell Pepper
  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Spices
  • Spring Onions

Preparation

  • Add all your dry ingredients to a bowl first to aviod lumps.
  • Then add your milk and butter to the flour and knead the dough on a flat surface.
  • Cut your dough into half and keep set one aside
  • Keep kneading the dough You would know when your dough is good when its less sticky
  • Sprinkle flour on the surface and keep kneading the dough.
  • Stop when its not sticky
  • Cover and set aside. Then start preparing your toppings.

    • Toppings Preparation

  • Wrinse your pepper and boil it in water. It's being boiled first before blending because its going to be added directly on your pizza.
  • Chop your bell pepper, onions, and spring onions
  • Boil you meat with no water, add onions, fannesse seed, seasoning and cover it to boil.
  • Dice and fry the hotdogs with no seasoning, then blend your boiled and unseasoned boiled pepper.
  • Dice your onions really tiny so as to caramelize it
  • Cook the blended pepper and add all the necessary seasonings to the peppersoup, suya spice, sweet chilli sauce, maggi and salt.
  • Make sure your mozzarella cheese is in a fridge or freezer. Chop it into strips and set aside.
  • Cut your dough into small pieces and use your rolling pin to make it flat, but not too thin.
  • Add your pizza dough to the oven and add your toppings with the shredded cheese, then put in the oven.
  • Leave for some minutes in the and then remove from the oven
  • Enjoy your fresh pizza!!!
