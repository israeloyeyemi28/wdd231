Payment terms:

  • Payment is due within 2 weeks of receipt/ invoice.
  • Accepted payment methods: Bank transfer, credit card, or check.
  • A late payment fee of 2% per month or maximum permitted by law will be applied to unpaid balances.
  • Payment id required for all scheduled services, even if cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice.
  • A deposit of $7.5 is required to secure scheduling, refundable only if carebuddies cancels services.

Cancellation policy:

  • If the client cancelsa sheduled service with less than 24 hours' notice, they will be charged a fee of 50% of the scheduled service fee.

  • In the event of Cancellation by CareBuddies, the client will be refunded any prepaid fees for cancelled services.
